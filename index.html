The challenge is simple: you have a ballroom competition you need to run. We make the solution simple as well. We provide all of the tools you need to run a competition, from registering competitors and setting up events to on deck check-in and managing callbacks during the event. By providing hardware and software to suit your needs, we make it easy to run a competition in a way that saves effort and trouble for organizers, competitors, and judges, while providing the flexibility to run your competition the way you want.
